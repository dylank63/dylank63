Pasta Bake
Keeps for 3 days.
Cost:€8

Ingredients: 1 white onion, , 1 red pepper, 1 yellow pepper, 1 clove of garlic,
2 chicken fillets, 1 tub of passata, 2 slices of bacon, 240g penne pasta, grated mozzarella.

Prep:
  1. Peel and chop onion.
  2. Chop peppers.
  3. Dice chicken and bacon.
Method:
  1. Preheat oven to 170Â°, heat pan with small amount of oil on medium heat, fill medium sized
    pot halfway and bring to boil and a small pot with minimal oil also on medium heat.
  2. Place half of chopped onion in pan and fry for 3 minutes, add chicken and bacon and fry until cooked through.
  3. Add peppers and fry for 3 minutes.
  4. Add pasta to pot with boiling water and cook for 8 minutes.
  5. Add second half of onion and garlic to small pot and fry off for 3 minutes, add passata and heat thoroughly, blend through and add to pan.
  6. Strain pasta and add to pan, mix all together.
  7. Transfer to baking dish, cover with mozzarella and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Allow to cool before transferring to fridge.

Lime and sea salt chicken wraps
Keeps for 4 days.
Cost:€11

Ingredients: 4 chicken breasts, 2 limes, sea salt, 1 tbsp sunflower oil, tortilla wraps, babygem lettuce,
1 red onion, chilli powder, mayonaise.

Prep:
  1. Cut chicken breasts into 5 strips from each.
  2. Juice and zest both limes.
  3. Mix chilli powder and mayo to create chilli mayo.
  4. Slice red onion.
  5. Peel and wash babygem lettuce.
Method:
  1. Heat oil in pan. When oil is hot add chicken strips, lime zest, sea salt and pepper.
  2. Heat tortilla wrap in microwave for 20-30 seconds.
  3. Cook chicken thoroughly.
  4. Add chilli mayo, red onion, babygem lettuce and chicken to the wrap.
  5. Fold wrap and slice in half.
  6. Serve hot.

Chicken fried rice
Keeps for 4 days.
Cost:€7

Ingredients: 1 cup of rice, 3 chicken breasts, 1 white onion, ½ cup of frozen peas, 4 carrots,
2 eggs, 3 tsp of cooking oil, soy sauce

Prep:
  1. Roast chicken in oven at 180° and cook for approx. 30 minutes.
  2. Dice chicken and set aside.
  3. Peel and fine dice carrot.
  4. Peel and fine dice onion.
  5. Peel and wash babygem lettuce.
Method:
  1. Place 1 cup of rice into 2 parts boiling water. Cook for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil over medium heat.
  3. Add peas, carrots and onion and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Crack 2 eggs into pan and mix through veg.
  5. Strain rice and add to pan.
  6. Add chicken and soy sauce.
  7. Allow to heat through evenly.

Mild Lamb Curry
Keeps for 3 days.
Cost:€13

Ingredients: 400g lamb shoulder, ¼ tbsp tumeric powder, ¼ tbsp curry powder, salt, pepper, 1 tbsp oil,
½ white onion, 1 red pepper, 2tbsp thick greek style yogurt.

Prep:
  1. Peel and dice onion.
  2. Dice pepper.
  3. Dice lamb shoulder.
Method:
  1. Place lamb, curry powder and tumeric powder into bowl and coat. Add salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil on medium heat, add coated lamb and cook for approx. 2 minutes or until browned.
  3. Add onion and pepper and fry for 4-5 mins.
  4. Add yogurt and allow to simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Can be served with naan bread or poppadoms.

Biryani beef curry
Keeps for 4 days.
Cost:€11

Ingredients: 2.5lbs of beef rib, 1tsp ginger spice, chicken stock, 1 clove of garlic,
vegetable oil, spiced tomato sauce.

Prep:
  1. Remove beef from bone and trim excess fat off.
  2. Fine dice garlic.
Method:
  1. Place beef in a large pot with garlic and ginger.
  2. Bring to boil, reduce and allow to simmer until tender for 3 to 4 hours.
  3. Remove beef from pot and allow to dry for 3 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in pan over medium heat.
  5. Seer beef until browned all over.
  6. Shred the beef using 2 forks.
  7. Put pot on low heat, add beef and tomato sauce. Cover and simmer for 15 mins.
  8. Serve hot.

Fiji style duck
Keeps for 3 days.
Cost:€11

Ingredients: 2 duck breasts, 1 white onion, garlic-ginger paste, ¼ tsp cumin spice, 2 sun dried chillies, 2 fresh red chillies, ¼ tsp chilli powder, 1 tbsp tumeric, ¼ cup of veg oil, ½ lime

Prep:
  1. Chop fresh chillies.
  2. Chop duck breast
  3. Dice white onion.
Method:
  1. Heat oil on high heat.
  2. Add dried chillies, cumin and fresh chillies. Allow to cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add onion and salt and cook.
  4. Ad tumeric powder and garlic and stir.
  5. Add duck.
  6. Squeeze half a lime and add salt to taste.
  7. Lower heat to medium and allow to simmer until tender.
  8. Serve with rice.
